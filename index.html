How it works

The device will be placed in the car in place of the music system. Music can still play with the help of speakers and bluetooth.

01

Proximity
Sensors

Our sensors help you avoid unnecessary damage by warning you when you are getting too close to another object. It works by a technique called the echo sounder to detect obstacles, much like sonar. The pulsing warning tone gets faster the closer you are to an obstacle. The system is equipped with ultrasonic sensors , they are small and can be painted to match the color of the vehicle so they blend in and are barely noticeable.

02

Driver Alcohol
Detection system

The system detects the amount of alcohol that is consumed by the driver of the vehicle. The system aims at preventing the user from driving when drunk and thereby intends to reduce the number of accidents occurring due to drunk driving.

03

High Speed
Alert System

This feature coordinates the car’s position, via GPS, with a database of speed limit information to alert drivers if they’re speeding. This helps drivers maintain a safe driving speed.

04

Driver Drowsiness
Detection System

Drowsiness detection is a safety technology that can prevent accidents that are caused by drivers who fell asleep while driving. Drowsiness detection system will detect if a person’s eyes are closed for a few seconds. This system will alert the driver when drowsiness is detected.

05

Intelligent Accident detection
and Alert System

This system aims to automatically detect an accident and quickly inform the emergency services and concerned family members with precise location through Short Message Service (SMS).
