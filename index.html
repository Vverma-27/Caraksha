How it works

The device will be placed in the car in place of the music system. Music can still play with the help of speakers and bluetooth.

1

Proximity

Connected speakers with sensors so that a warning of TOO CLOSE can be given to the drivers (can have an off switch if lanes are narrow and the beeping goes on)

2

Alcohol

If alcohol is consumed by the driver, there will be beeping sounds in the car (no off switch). SOS will be sent to the family members if the driver decides to drink and drive

3

Speeding

If car is going at high speed and the cars are very close, the cars will stop If speed is medium high and the car makes a hard brake, the car behind will automatically stop too (assuming it has the device too) SOS will be sent to the family members if the driver over speeds

4

Drowsiness

If the driver falls asleep (closes his/ her eyes for more than a few seconds) or takes his/ her eyes off the road for more than a few seconds then the camera will detect it and the speaker will start beeping

5

Accidents

If there will be an accident, the health card of the general driver/s of the car will be displayed on the dashboard SOS will be sent to family members and ambulance will be contacted as well

Why Caraksha

Our product aims to provide a safety mechanism that could improve driver safety as well as the safety of the citizens on the road.
We identified three main reasons for car accidents in India-

Distracted driving
drunk driving
overspeeding

Our product aims to reduce this by adding a device in the car at minimum cost that helps improve road safety.

1

Prevention

This will prevent road accidents from occurring.

2

Portable

These will be portable so they’ll be able to connect these in their old vehicles.

3

Distraction

It prevents over speeding and getting distracted while driving.

4

Medical Aid

In case of accidents time won’t be wasted in calling for an ambulance and health record of the person would be available on the screen
